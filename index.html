How to Play Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

Game Rules:

  • The game consists of 9 small Tic-Tac-Toe boards arranged in a 3Ã—3 large board.
  • Winning a small board follows normal Tic-Tac-Toe rules (3 in a row).
  • To win the game, you must win 3 small boards in a row on the large board.
  • The cell you play determines which small board your opponent must play in next.
  • If that board is already full or won, your opponent may choose any available board.

Strategy Tips:

  • Focus on both winning small boards and controlling where your opponent can play.
  • Try to send your opponent to boards you've already won or that are disadvantageous for them.
  • Think ahead - consider not just your immediate move but also where it will force your opponent to play next.
